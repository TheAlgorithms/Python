12 A Red-Black tree, which is a self-balancing BST (binary search 

13 tree). 

14 This tree has similar performance to AVL trees, but the balancing is 

15 less strict, so it will perform faster for writing/deleting nodes 

16 and slower for reading in the average case, though, because they're 

17 both balanced binary search trees, both will get the same asymptotic 

18 performance. 

19 To read more about them, https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Red–black_tree 

20 Unless otherwise specified, all asymptotic runtimes are specified in 

21 terms of the size of the tree.
